Hi,

I am a PostDoc in the Infectious Diseases department headed by prof. Evelina Tacconelli in the University of Verona, I'm focused on limiting the emergence of antibiotic resistance. My work is highly transversal, involving close collaboration between clinical experts, statisticians, and data experts.

I believe that effective communication in interdisciplinary teams requires a conscious democratic process, and I'm passionate about teaching and scientific data visualization to facilitate this process. I'm currently managing and involved in several interdisciplinary tasks within the EU-funded Orchestra. project, which is dedicated to developing innovative solutions for combatting antibiotic resistance.

My past and current projects span a diverse range of approaches including mata-analysis, clinical metagenomics, clinical data analysis and management, and molecular dynamics. Additionally, I'm involved in related scientific visualisation, and teaching.
