Hi,

My work is focused on limiting the emergence of antibiotic resistance. Still, it is highly transversional concerning the methods and data. Currently, I'm a PostDoc in the Infectious Diseases department headed by prof. Evelina Tacconelli in the University of Verona.

All of my projects require close collaboration between clinical experts, statisticians and data experts. I believe communication in interdisciplinary teams should be conducted as a conscious democratic process, although it often takes more time. Therefore, I am interested in teaching and scientific data visualisation. I am also managing and involved in several interdisciplinary tasks within EU-funded Orchestra.

Here, I would like to present the scope of my past and current projects across: mata-analysis, clinical metagenomics, clinical data analysis and management, and molecular dynamics, as well as other related activities: scientific visualisation, teaching and some other small pieces of writing .

Ania
